Geovisualization Portfolio GEOG 5540

Median Age of Males in Oregon

This is a choropleth map depicting the median age of males by county in the State of Oregon. There is not a large spatial pattern being exhibited. However, there is a slight trend of lower ages near the metropolitan areas of the state.

Professional/Scientific Employees in Oregon

This is a proportional symbol map depicting the number of Professional/Scientific employees by county in the state of Oregon. The larger circles in the NW region of the state are near where Portland and other larger cities are. Going south from there are major university towns of Corvalis and Eugene, which is where Oregon State University and University of Oregon are located, respectively.

Median Household Income and % White Population in Oregon

This is a bivariate choropleth map depicting the relationship between median household income and percentage of white population. There is a general spatial pattern showing higher median income levels in the metropolitan areas, with lower values in rural areas. There are also lower levels of white population in the metropolitan areas, but they do not mirror exactly the distribution seen with income levels.
